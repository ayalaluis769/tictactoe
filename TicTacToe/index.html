Welcome to Tic Tac Toe!

    The rules are simple:

  1. One player is "X" while the other is "O"
  2. To win, a player simply has to get 3 of their symbol in row
  3. A row can be made either vertically, horizonatally, or diagonally
  4. If the game ends in a draw the board will announce "DRAW" and reset itself
